Byriani

"Description"

Byriani is a heart favourite dish of Pakistanis and its going places now. Byriani has found itself in the hearts of people all across the Globe now.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Chicken
  • aloo bakhara
  • oil for cooking
  • Byriani masala
  • rice
  • salt
  • green chilies
  • yellow food color


Steps

  1. Fry onions in oil
  2. when golden , add ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add chicken
  4. cook till golden and all water is dried
  5. Add tomatoes
  6. Add byriani masala
  7. Add aloo bakhara
  8. cook well till a nice masla is made
